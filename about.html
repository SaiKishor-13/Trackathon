About Trackathon

Trackathon is an innovative budgeting platform built to empower students to manage their finances smartly. Our goal is to make financial literacy accessible, interactive, and actionable for young minds.

We believe that saving and spending should be intuitive, insightful, and sustainable. Trackathon helps users track their expenses, set budgets, and make informed decisions — all in a visually engaging and user-friendly way.

Our Vision

To create a financially aware generation capable of managing resources wisely and achieving long-term stability.

Our Mission

To simplify expense tracking through design, technology, and user-focused innovation.

Our Values

Innovation, Integrity, and Impact — we aim to inspire responsible spending habits that make a difference.
